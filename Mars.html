Mars Facts And Figures:

The length of a day on Mars is 24hr 37min 23sec.

Mars' average distance from the Sun is 227.3 million km.

The surface gravity of Mars is 38% that of Earth.

A Martian year is 686.98 Earth days.

Mars has four seasons- Summer, Autumn, Winter and Spring.

Mars has ice caps at the north and south poles.
